Mini Golf

Description

"Head over to the counter to grab your putters and golf balls. This is a good opportunity for some playful banter or light-hearted competition as you choose your equipment. Course Exploration: Take a stroll around the course together, checking out the various holes and obstacles. Enjoy the scenery and chat about your favorite holes or any strategies you might have. Gameplay: Take turns teeing off at each hole, encouraging each other and celebrating successes. Don't be afraid to laugh at your own missed shots or celebrate lucky shots together. Friendly Competition: Keep track of scores if you're both competitive, but remember to keep it light-hearted and fun. A little friendly rivalry can add excitement to the date, but it's more about enjoying each other's company than winning."
