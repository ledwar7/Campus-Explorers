Ice Skating

Description

Meeting Up: Start by meeting at the ice skating rink. Choose a cozy meeting spot nearby where you can grab a warm drink or snack before hitting the ice.

Renting Skates: If you or your date don't have ice skates, you can rent them at the rink. It's a fun experience to pick out skates together and help each other put them on.

On the Ice: Once you're all laced up, take to the ice! Hold hands as you glide around the rink, enjoying the feeling of movement and the chill in the air. Don't worry if one of you is a beginner; it can be endearing to teach each other and laugh at any slips or wobbles.
