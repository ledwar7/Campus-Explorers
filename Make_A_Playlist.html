The theme will influence the selection of songs. Curating the Playlist: Once I have an idea of preferences and theme, I'd start curating the playlist. I'd choose a mix of songs that align with the mood and resonate with both of us. This might include some classic love songs, upbeat tunes, or tracks that hold special meaning."
