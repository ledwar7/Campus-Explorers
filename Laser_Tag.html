Laser Tag

Description

"Once the game starts, get ready for some adrenaline-pumping action! You and your date will navigate through the dimly lit arena, dodging obstacles and opponents while trying to tag as many targets as possible. Teamwork: You can strategize and work together with your date to outsmart the other players. Communication and coordination can help you dominate the game! Friendly Competition: While you'll both be focused on winning, remember to keep it light and fun. Cheer each other on, share a few laughs, and don't take the game too seriously."
